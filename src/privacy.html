Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 19, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use Lunch Bunch, we collect:

  • Account information: Your Google account name, email address, and profile photo (provided via Google Sign-In).
  • Usage data: Your votes, group memberships, and app interactions stored in our database.
  • Device tokens: Push notification tokens for delivering voting reminders and winner announcements.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and operate the App's voting and group management features.
  • Send push notifications (voting reminders and winner announcements) if you opt in.
  • Send email notifications to group managers about membership requests.
  • Improve the App and fix issues.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore) servers located in the United States. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Firebase/Google: For authentication, database, and push notification services.
  • RevenueCat: For subscription management (anonymous app user IDs only).
  • SendGrid: For transactional email delivery to group managers.

5. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us. Group managers can remove members from their groups at any time.

6. Push Notifications

You can enable or disable push notifications in the App's settings. Disabling notifications will stop voting reminders and winner announcements.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the App.
