Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a group?

Tap the + button on the home screen, name your group, and share the invite link with your lunch crew.

How do I invite people?

Open your group, tap "Invite," and share the link via text, email, or any messaging app.

When does voting end?

Each daily vote has a deadline. You'll get a push notification reminder before it closes and another when the winner is announced.

Can I change my vote?

Yes! You can change your vote as many times as you want before the deadline. Just tap a different restaurant.

What is Lunch Bunch Manager?

The free version lets you participate in one group. Lunch Bunch Manager ($9.99/year) unlocks unlimited groups — great if you have multiple lunch crews.

How do I manage my subscription?

Your subscription is managed through your Apple ID. Go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions on your device.

How do I delete my account?

Contact us at support@lunchbunch.app and we will delete your account and all associated data.
